About Mocha

We're building the future of
how software gets made.

Mocha is a platform for agentic development — where AI doesn't just suggest code, it ships it. We're a small team with a big belief: the best days of software are still ahead of us.

"Every developer deserves a brilliant collaborator."

We started Mocha because we were exhausted. Not tired from working hard — tired of working on the wrong things. Boilerplate. Wiring. Config files. The thousand tiny tasks that stand between a good idea and a working product.

AI promised to fix this. But the early tools felt more like autocomplete on steroids than a real collaborator. They helped you type faster — they didn't help you build faster.

Mocha is our answer to that gap. It's an agentic platform that doesn't just generate code — it understands context, makes decisions, and executes entire workflows on your behalf. It's the kind of collaborator that comes in on a Sunday night and says "I took care of that thing."

We believe AI agents aren't a novelty. They're the next fundamental layer of software development. And we're here to make sure that layer is fast, reliable, and — above all — delightful to work with.

Our story

2023 Q1
The idea

Alex and Sara had been building software professionally for over a decade each. They were frustrated by the same things — not the hard problems, but the boring ones. They started sketching what an AI that could actually act would look like. The napkin drawing is still on the office wall.

2023 Q3
First prototype

Mocha v0.1 was built over a single weekend in a rented Airbnb with poor WiFi and excellent coffee. It could clone a GitHub repo, understand its structure, and make a small but real code change autonomously. It worked. Barely — but it worked.

2024 Q1
Launch

We posted on a developer forum at 11pm on a Tuesday. By Thursday morning, over 1,000 developers had signed up. Our servers melted twice. We didn't sleep much. We've never been happier.

2026 Q1
Series A

We raised $24M to pour into what we've always cared about: making Mocha faster, smarter, and more reliable for the developers who depend on it every day. The mission hasn't changed — the runway just got a lot longer.

What we believe

🔓
Build in public

We share our thinking, our mistakes, and our roadmap. We believe transparency builds trust — and that the developer community deserves to see how the sausage is made, even when it's messy.

⌨️
Developer-first always

Every decision goes through one filter: is this actually better for the developer? Not the org chart, not the pitch deck — the person sitting at a keyboard trying to ship something meaningful.

⚡
Move fast, stay thoughtful

We move quickly — but not recklessly. Speed and care aren't opposites. The best teams we've seen do both at once, and that's what we aspire to be every day.

✨
Make it delightful

Good enough isn't good enough. We care about the details that make something feel alive — the animation that's just right, the error message that's actually helpful, the onboarding that feels like a welcome, not a chore.
